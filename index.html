About Us

In reverence to the sacred teachings of the Christian faith and the divine connection of humanity to the Tree of Life, this trust embodies organic unity, community engagement, and resource sovereignty. Inspired by the philosophy of the Gospels, this plan seeks to nurture a network of legacies built on unified creation and craftsmanship.

Purpose and Vision

The Trust of Arbor Vitae is founded on the principle of fostering harmony and cooperation among individuals and communities. It aims to promote social and environmental responsibility, inspire creativity and craftsmanship, and support resource sovereignty for the benefit of present and future generations.

Objectives

  • Broaden the scope to include local and global outreach.
  • Develop specific initiatives promoting environmental responsibility.
  • Break down the purpose into actionable objectives.

Beneficiaries

Our beneficiaries include individuals, families, and communities who share our vision and objectives.

  • Clear criteria for becoming a beneficiary.

Distribution of Assets

The assets of the Trust of Arbor Vitae are managed and utilized in a manner that aligns with its core principles and objectives.

  • Develop a detailed strategic plan for asset distribution.
  • Provide detailed financial reports and updates.

Legacy Building

The trust aims to serve as a model for other families of faith to establish their own private trusts, fostering a network of interconnected legacies.

  • Actively seek to expand the network by partnering with other trusts and organizations.
  • Provide comprehensive documentation and resources to help others establish similar trusts.

Administration

The trust is governed by the laws of the relevant state or country, with provisions for severability, amendment, and termination.

  • Invest in administrative tools and technologies to streamline operations.
  • Conduct regular reviews of the trust's administration to identify areas for improvement.

Community Engagement

The trust emphasizes community engagement and resource sovereignty.

  • Develop and implement community programs that address local needs.
  • Establish robust feedback mechanisms to gather input from the community and beneficiaries.

Educational Initiatives

The trust's principles include promoting educational opportunities and supporting learning institutions.

  • Create specific educational trusts to fund scholarships, educational materials, and school programs.
  • Partner with educational institutions to develop collaborative learning programs.
