Preamble

In reverence to the sacred teachings of the Christian faith and the divine connection of humanity to the Tree of Life, this Trust of Arbor Vitae is established to embody organic unity, community engagement, and resource sovereignty. Inspired by the philosophy of the Gospels, this trust seeks to nurture a network of legacies built on unified creation and craftsmanship.

Article 1: Purpose

The Trust of Arbor Vitae is founded on the principle of fostering harmony and cooperation among individuals and communities. It aims to promote social and environmental responsibility, inspire creativity and craftsmanship, and support resource sovereignty for the benefit of present and future generations.

Article 2: Trustees

2.1 Appointment of Trustees:
The initial Trustees of the Trust of Arbor Vitae shall be appointed as follows: [List Initial Trustees].

2.2 Powers of Trustees:
The Trustees shall have the power to manage, invest, and distribute the assets of the Trust in accordance with the purposes set forth herein and shall act in accordance with the teachings of the Gospels and exercise their fiduciary duties with diligence and care.

2.3 Removal and Replacement of Trustees:
A Trustee may resign by delivering written notice to the remaining Trustees. The Trustees may remove a Trustee by unanimous vote (excluding the Trustee in question). In the event of a vacancy, the remaining Trustees shall appoint a successor who upholds the values and intentions of this Trust.

2.4 Decision Making:
Decisions by the Trustees shall be made by majority vote, except as otherwise specified in this document.

Article 3: Beneficiaries

3.1 Designation of Beneficiaries:
The beneficiaries of the Trust of Arbor Vitae shall include individuals, families, and communities who share the vision and objectives of this trust.

3.2 Rights of Beneficiaries:
Beneficiaries shall have no legal claim to the assets of the Trust, but may benefit from the distributions made at the discretion of the Trustees in accordance with the Trust’s purposes.

Article 4: Distribution of Assets

4.1 Management of Assets:
The assets of the Trust of Arbor Vitae shall be managed and utilized in a manner that aligns with its core principles and objectives.

4.2 Allocation of Resources:
Resources shall be allocated to projects and initiatives that promote sustainable practices, artisanal skills, and community development, in accordance with the teachings of the Gospels.

4.3 Accounting and Reporting:
The Trustees shall maintain accurate records of the Trust’s finances and activities. Annual reports shall be prepared and made available to the beneficiaries and any other relevant parties.

Article 5: Legacy Building

5.1 Network of Trusts:
The Trust of Arbor Vitae shall serve as a model for other families of faith to establish their own private trusts, fostering a network of interconnected legacies rooted in shared values and beliefs.

5.2 Encouragement of Collaboration:
By creating a framework for collaborative creation and community empowerment, this trust seeks to inspire and uplift future generations.

Article 6: Administration

6.1 Governing Law:
This Trust shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of [State/Country].

6.2 Severability:
If any provision of this Trust is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

6.3 Amendment:
This Trust may be amended by a unanimous vote of the Trustees, provided that any such amendment is consistent with the Trust’s purpose and principles.

6.4 Termination:
The Trust may be terminated by a unanimous vote of the Trustees. Upon termination, any remaining assets shall be distributed in a manner that aligns with the Trust’s purposes and objectives, as determined by the Trustees.

Conclusion

In establishing The Trust of Arbor Vitae, we commit to honoring the sacred bond between humanity and the Tree of Life, as well as the teachings of the Gospels. Through our dedication to organic unity, community engagement, craftsmanship, and resource sovereignty, we aspire to build a sustainable and inclusive legacy that embodies the spirit of love, compassion, and unity as exemplified by the life and teachings of Jesus Christ.
